Meet Jin

Jin is your friendly Slack bot designed to help you manage your tasks and interactions within Slack. With Jin, you can keep track of your messages, manage your to-do lists, and more!

Here are some commands you can use with Jin:

  • /ping: Check if Jin is active in your channel.
  • /help: Get a list of available commands.
  • /message-count: See how many messages you've sent in the channel.
  • /previous-messages: View your previous messages in the channel.
  • /add [item]: Add an item to your list.
  • /list: List all items in your list.
  • /find [item]: Find an item in your list.
  • /remove [item]: Remove an item from your list.
  • /clear: Clear all items from your list.
  • /update [item] [new_item]: Update an existing item to a new one.
